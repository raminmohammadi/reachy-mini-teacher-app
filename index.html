powered by Google Gemini Live and a fully local AI pipeline.

💬

Default Profile

A warm Farsi-speaking AI companion for everyday conversation, with real-time head tracking, expressive emotions, and live weather lookup.

🧑‍🏫

English Teacher

A structured 7-unit curriculum designed for elderly Persian speakers learning English. Conversational, patient, and progress-aware across sessions.

How It Works

1
Connect — The app connects to your Reachy Mini over USB or Wi-Fi.
2
Listen — Real-time audio streams from the robot's microphone to the AI backend.
3
Think — Gemini Live (or the local Whisper + Ollama pipeline) generates a response and selects robot actions.
4
Respond — The robot speaks, moves its head, plays an emotion, and remembers the conversation for next time.

Built-in Tools

🎯 Head tracking
😊 Emotions & dance
🌤️ Live weather (Open-Meteo)
📷 Camera & scene description
🔀 Profile switching
💾 Session memory
